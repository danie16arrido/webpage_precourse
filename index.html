C-3PO

Photos

Hi my name us C-3PO, my friends call me Threepio for short, I am a protocol droid specifically designed to interact with organics.

My TranLang III Communicator module combined with my AA-1 VerboBrain allows me to be fluent in over six million forms of communication. I am equipped with over thirty secondary functions.

I usually hang out with my mate R2-D2, a small, wheeled R2-series astromech droid.

My main function as a protocol droid is to assist with etiquette, customs, and translation so that meetings of different cultures ran smoothly.

I have loyally served over forty masters. I hate adventure and space travel, as they often resulted in me losing parts, and having them reattached, sometimes incorrectly.
